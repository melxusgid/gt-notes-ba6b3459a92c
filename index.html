Version 1.1.0 — round two of your notes Latest

Pushed today

Everything you wrote in your last bit of feedback, sorted. The rings are cuter now too.

Fixes

  • The rings close all the way now. If you haven't built a morning or evening routine yet, that ring counts as closed instead of sitting empty forever. And the skin-care ring closes when you change your pillowcase — logging symptoms is bonus progress on top.
  • The "more" menu on someone else's profile (the ··· in the top right with Block / Report) wasn't expanding when tapped. Fixed.

New

  • Calendar dots per treatment. Instead of the whole day being one color, each day shows a little colored dot for every treatment scheduled that day. So days with two treatments show two dots, days with three show three, and so on. The legend lists each treatment by name and color.
  • Tap any day on the calendar to open a day-detail view: what was scheduled, how much of the routine got done, the pillowcase toggle, and a notes field you can edit right there.
  • Notes attached to each day. There's a notes box at the bottom of the Today page now. Scroll to a different day and you'll see (and can edit) that day's notes. Same notes show up if you tap that day on the calendar — they're the same place, just two ways in.
  • A little heart in the middle of the rings. It fills in when all three rings close. Just a small cute thing.

Little tweaks

  • The rings now match your theme. Instead of the old fixed peach/purple/mint, the three rings are three shades of whichever pastel theme you picked. They restyle themselves when you change themes.
  • Text takes on your theme color. In light mode, body text shifts to a darker shade of your accent; in dark mode, a lighter shade. So the whole app feels colored by the palette you chose, not just the buttons.

To get this update

Open the TestFlight app on your phone. There'll be an Update button next to GlowTracker. Tap it and you're good.

Version 1.0.1 — your first round of feedback

The build before this one

All the things you flagged in round one.

Fixes

  • Tapping to check things off works instantly now. The tap was always saving, but the screen wasn't refreshing in time. That's sorted.
  • Adding routines and treatments shows up right away instead of feeling laggy.
  • The calendar uses a different color for each treatment so you can tell them apart at a glance. The legend now lists each one by name.
  • Recent logs in the Symptom Log are tappable. Tap any past day to review or edit what you wrote.

New

  • Three new things in Settings. Open Settings (the gear at the top right) and you'll find an appearance picker (system / light / dark), a Help page that walks through every tab, and a button to revisit the onboarding tutorial whenever you want.
  • A Help page with a quick tour of every tab.
  • One-tap "restart the tutorial" in case you ever want to see the onboarding again.

Little tweaks

  • The skin care ring (the third one) now tells you what fills it — logging how your skin feels + changing your pillowcase.

The very first build

Your first invite

This is what you started with.

What's in there

  • Today — your daily morning and evening checklists, with little built-in timers between steps.
  • Treatments — add your own actives (retinoids, acids, vitamin C, whatever), pick the days you use them, optionally with a gradual ramp-up.
  • Closing rings like Apple Fitness — morning, evening, and a third for everyday care. Try to close all three.
  • Calendar showing which nights are treatment nights at a glance.
  • Progress with streaks, a 5-week heatmap, milestones, and a skin-condition trend graph.
  • Journal for weekly progress photos — first vs. latest comparison built in.
  • Symptom Log for redness, peeling, dryness, irritation, and breakouts.
  • Social — profiles, messaging, and borrowing routine steps from friends.
  • Six pastel themes, light and dark mode, and a cozy dark mode that isn't harsh.
  • Onboarding with a hands-on tutorial so you know how it all works.
